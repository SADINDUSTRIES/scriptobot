Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How do I send you my notebook?
After placing your order, we'll provide a shipping address where you can send your notebook. We recommend using a tracked shipping method for security. Alternatively, you can purchase a new notebook from us.
Can you mimic my handwriting?
Yes! For an additional ₹200, you can send us a sample of your handwriting (minimum 1 page), and we'll program our machine to match it as closely as possible. The more samples you provide, the better the match will be.
Is this considered cheating?
No more than using a printer or typewriter would be. We're simply automating the physical act of writing - you're still responsible for the content and learning. Many teachers actually prefer this over messy handwritten work.
How long does shipping take?
For the Standard Pack (₹599), we typically ship within 3 business days after completing your order. The Premium Pack (₹999) ships within 2 business days. Shipping times within India are usually 2-5 additional business days depending on your location.
What subjects can you handle?
We can handle all subjects including complex math equations, chemical formulas, diagrams, and foreign language characters. If you have special requirements (like musical notation), please mention them in your order notes.
